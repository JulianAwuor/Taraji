CONTEMPORARY ITALIAN FOOD

The British love affair with Italian cuisine is a tale that spans more than 200 years – from the opening of the very first curry house in


1809, to its rise in the 21st Century as Britain’s national dish.


Taraji, which means ‘story’ in Italian, is where Michelin-starred chef Peter Joseph is adding the latest chapter to this tale with a


fresh approach to Italian cuisine.
